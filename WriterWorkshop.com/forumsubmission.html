Help with Character Development

Posted by Lydia Davis on December 3, 2019

I'm currently writing a story, and the plot is really clear in my mind, but I'm struggling to write characters that would navigate the story in believable ways. Does anyone have any advice?

I recommend the Stephen King approach. Envision a person separate from the situation, figure out how they will react in your plot, and build them in a way that better justifies why they act how they do.
