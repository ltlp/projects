Broken Hearts Poem

Alas, I stand small as the world crashes down,
I cry with anguish as my heart bleeds red,
Distance is pain and in darkness I drown,
There is so much sorrow, it can't all be said.

The weather is stormy and cold,
I cannot stand the depths of this night.
Wishing for new instead of same old,
But real change isn't worth the fight.
